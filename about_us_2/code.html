Our Journey

From a modest regional debate circle to a national beacon for intellectual excellence.

"The future is built one argument at a time."

2012 — THE GENESIS

A Shared Vision

Founded by a group of five collegiate champions in a small library hall, the Scholarch began as a regional hub for training elite public speakers.

2016 — REGIONAL ASCENT

The First Grand Open

Expansion to five major cities across the province. Launched our signature "Scholarch Invitational," which attracted over 200 institutions in its inaugural year.

2020 — DIGITAL EVOLUTION

Bridging Frontiers

Pivoted to a global hybrid model, training over 10,000 students through our proprietary digital debate framework during the global shift to remote learning.

2024 — NATIONAL AUTHORITY

The Scholarch Mandate

Recognized as the primary influence in national debate curriculum development, shaping the next generation of intellectual leaders.
